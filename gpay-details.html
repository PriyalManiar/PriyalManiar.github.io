Predictive Analytics for Marketing Campaigns with a Study on GPay Campaigns

  • Tech Stack:
    • Programming Language and Tools : Python, Jupyter Notebooks
    • Libraries and Frameworks : Scikit-Learn, Pandas, Numpy, Seaborn, Matplotlib,
  • Concepts:
    • Supervised Learning Algorithms : Decision Tree, Random Forest, Support Vector Machine, Logistic Regression
    • Feature Engineering and Model Evaluation
    • Data Visualization : Tableau

Project Overview

  • A study to explore the use of predictive analytics in Customer Relationship Management (CRM) by leveraging machine learning classifiers to determine the factors influencing the success of marketing campaigns

  • Built predictive models on survey data collected for 100 students to evaluate the likeability of Google Pay offers and promotions among college students.

  • Designed a Tableau dashboard to visualize factors driving campaign success - mapping the relations between promotional elements and customer preferences, aiding data-driven marketing strategies.
