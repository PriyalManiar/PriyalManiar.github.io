AI Autosegmentation of LAD Artery in Radiotherapy for Left Breast Cancer

2024

Tag 1 Tag 2 Tag 3
Publication link

Aim: Evaluate auto-segmentation of the Left Anterior Descending (LAD) artery in radiotherapy planning to reduce observer variability and cardiac complications (DICOM CT, 9 patients).

Approach: Compared edge detection, region growing, atlas-based, and anatomical landmark methods. Landmark-based approach identified as most promising.

In partnership with Nanavati Max Super Speciality Hospital, Mumbai.
