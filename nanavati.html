AI based Autosegmentation of LAD in Treatment of Left Breast Cancer

  • Programming Language and Platform: Python, Google Colab
  • Tools & Frameworks:
    • Medical Image Processing : py_dicom, dicom_contour, scikit-image, OpenCV
    • Algorithms : Edge Detection (Sobel & Roberts), Region-Growing, Atlas-based Ssegmentation
  • Publication Link: Indian Journal of Medical and Paediatric Oncology

Project Overview

  • Aim: Evaluate the feasibilty of auto-segmentation of the Left Anterior Descending (LAD) artery during radiotherapy planning in breast cancer patients to reduce inter and intra-observer variability and minimize chances of cardiac complications using the DICOM CT scans of 9 patients.

  • Challenges: The LAD artery’s small size (~5 pixels), high interpatient variability, and lack of distinctive features made segmentation difficult.

  • Approach: Compared multiple AI-based segmentation methods, including edge detection, region growing, atlas-based, extrapolation-based, and anatomical landmark-based approaches.

  • Findings: Traditional AI segmentation struggled due to the unpredictable path and variability of the LAD artery. The anatomical landmark–based approach was identified as a potential solution.

    • (Conducted the study in partnership with Nanavati Max Super Speciality Hospital, Mumbai, India.)
